Our Story

Techayz Limited was evolved in year 2019 in United Kingdom. Over the years, our team of expert has successfully completed software projects with a number of renowned companies Globally.

We offer Customized and Tailor-made solutions for various industries, eliminating the heavy infrastructure procurement cost and its regular management/administration costs, yet more versatile, user friendly, mobility enhanced and feature rich solutions.

Our solutions has supported the businesses to enhance their productivity, increase their profitability & growth. Our efficient Reporting Tools & Management Dashboards help management take Effective Business Decision for their Businesses. We offer complete integrated environment with our Tech modules hence, truly a One Window Solution Provider.
